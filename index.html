About Me

I’ve spent time recently turning coffee into responsive, user-centric web applications. My toolkit is built on the "Holy Trinity" of front-end development:

  • HTML: Building the semantic bones of the internet.
  • CSS: Making sure those bones look incredible (and stay responsive).
  • JavaScript: Bringing everything to life with logic and interactivity.

I’m passionate about the intersection of design and engineering—ensuring that every site I build isn't just a pretty face, but a fast, functional tool for the user. When I’m not debugging a tricky CSS layout, you can find me exploring new web standards and optimizing load times.
